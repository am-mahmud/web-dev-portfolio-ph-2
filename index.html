Hey,
I'm Joey Tribbiani

— an actor, sandwich enthusiast, and
expert in the art of “How you doin’?”,
bringing laughs and love.

I’ve spent a couple of decades… okay, more like a couple of seasons… acting,
learning what makes a story really click with people. co-run this place called.
The Workshop — it’s kind of like a studio, but fancier — where I collaborate on
big, brave ideas with some pretty awesome folks (and sometimes, sandwiches).

Lately, I’ve been thinking… maybe it’s not just about
the script or the set. Maybe what really makes
a brand — or a scene — work is the people.

So I’m looking to work with teams who’ve got the talent, the energy, the whole creative crew… but something just isn’t clicking. Maybe it’s the culture. Maybe the
vision's fuzzy. Maybe the director keeps eating my props. I don’t know.

Here's what you
can expect from
me ...

I'll bring colour, big energy and fresh thinking.

Learn, challenge and reconnect
the dots - joining your team
with conviction and openness.

Bridge the gap between creative and commercial objectives - speaking both languages.

Recognise potential - pushing people to become better than they ever thought possible.

Professional development and guided career advice -recognising and playing to each persons unique strengths.

Teach teams to move at velocity and have a hell of a lot of fun doing it.

I’ve been everywhere — New York (obviously), London (cool accents),
Miami (girls), Toronto (cold pizza), and now I’m living it up in Mallorca.
Don’t ask me to spell it.

Let’s get to work — after lunch, of course.
